PAW Patrol: Adventure Bay debt calculator

This is an attempt to calculate the cost of the PAW Patrol to the Adventure Bay taxpayer, as well as the debt incurred to the town. Since Ryder declined to share any cost data, against governmental regulations, all costs are estimated, but can be changed by the user of this tool. This calculator uses the following assumptions:
  • All figures in USD, 2020 economics.
  • Kit consists of communication devices, suit and equipment fitted to suit.
  • Cost of vehicles account for their advanced modularity technology.
  • Each year consists of 4 seasons.
  • Kits/vehicles/buildings do not get written off, but maintained ad infinitum (highly optimistic, particularly considering the growth rate of pups of this age and typical life of vehicles carrying canines).
  • The town does not have any other expenditures outside of the PAW Patrol (again, highly optimistic).
  • Debt repay period assumes an interest free loan for the whole debt and repay duration (again, highly optimistic).
